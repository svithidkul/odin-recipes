Recipe for making lasagna

A timeless favorite featuring layers of tender pasta, tangy tomato sauce, creamy b√©chamel, and a generous sprinkle of melted cheese, all baked to golden-brown perfection. A comforting culinary masterpiece that brings together rich flavors and textures in every bite, making it an ideal choice for gatherings or cozy nights in.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage (optional)
  • Tomato sauce or marinara sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Onion, finely chopped (optional)
  • Garlic, minced (optional)

Steps:

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until they are al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare your tomato sauce or marinara sauce if you're making it from scratch, or use store-bought.
  3. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce on the bottom of a baking dish to prevent sticking.
  4. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes.
  5. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before slicing.
