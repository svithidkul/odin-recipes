Recipe for making tuna macaroni salad

Tuna Macaroni Salad: A creamy blend of al dente macaroni, flaked tuna, crisp vegetables, and a tangy dressing. Perfect for picnics or as a quick meal.

Ingredients:

  • Macaroni pasta
  • Canned tuna, drained
  • Mayonnaise
  • Dijon mustard

Steps:

  1. Cook the macaroni pasta according to the package instructions until al dente.
  2. Drain and rinse under cold water to cool down the pasta. Set aside
  3. In a large salad bowl or individual serving plates, arrange a bed of mixed salad greens.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, combine mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, and lemon juice. Stir until well combined.
  5. Sprinkle toasted nuts or seeds over the salad for added crunch and texture.
